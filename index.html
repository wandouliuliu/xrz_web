Biography

I am a first year master student in Electrical Computer Engineering at University of California, Los Angeles. I am currently working as a volunteer research assistant at The Ozcan Research Group lead by Prof. Aydogan Ozcan. I work closely with Prof. Yair Rivenson. I obtained my B.Eng. degree with the highest honor (President Xu Teli’s Special Fellowship) from School of Automation, Beijing Institute of Technology supervised by Prof. Dawei Shi. I was fortunate to be a Mitacs-CSC visiting student in Servier Virtual Cardiac Centre at University of Alberta in summer 2018, supervised by Prof. Kumaradevan Punithakumar and Prof. Michelle Noga.

Research Interests

    My current research mainly focuses on developing deep learning-based techniques for biomedical imaging including registration and segmentation. I am also keen on exploring optimization and computer vision problems and open to new fields.
